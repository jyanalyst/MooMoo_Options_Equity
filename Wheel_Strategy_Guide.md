*Systematic Income Generation Through Cash-Secured Puts & Covered Calls*

Platform: MooMoo \| Market: US Stocks \| Capital: \$44,500 USD

STRATEGY OVERVIEW

The Wheel is a systematic income strategy that generates premium by
selling cash-secured puts on quality stocks you want to own. If
assigned, you sell covered calls to generate additional income and exit
at a profit. Either way, you collect premium.

  ------------------ ----------------------------------------------------
  **Core Thesis**    Implied volatility consistently overstates realized
                     volatility (avg 3.6 vol points). Selling options
                     harvests this premium while building positions in
                     quality companies.

  **Edge Source**    Insurance premium (puts protect against crashes),
                     fear of atypical events (people overestimate extreme
                     events), and skewness premium (puts are more
                     overpriced than calls).

  **Key Principle**  Only sell puts on stocks you genuinely want to own
                     at the strike price. Assignment is opportunity, not
                     failure.
  ------------------ ----------------------------------------------------

THE WHEEL CYCLE

Phase 1: Selling Cash-Secured Puts

You sell a put option and receive premium upfront. You must have cash to
buy 100 shares if assigned. If the stock stays above your strike, the
option expires worthless and you keep the premium. If assigned, you buy
the stock at your chosen price (strike) minus the premium received.

  ----------------------- ----------------------- -----------------------
  **Outcome**             **What Happens**        **Next Step**

  **Expires Worthless**   Stock stayed above      Keep premium, sell new
                          strike                  put

  Assigned                Stock fell below strike Own shares, move to
                                                  Phase 2
  ----------------------- ----------------------- -----------------------

Phase 2: Selling Covered Calls (If Assigned)

You now own 100 shares. Sell a call option above your cost basis to
collect additional premium. If the stock rises to your strike, shares
are called away at a profit. If not, you keep premium and sell another
call. OTS4 research shows you actually want the stock to be called
away - that means it rallied.

  ----------------------- ----------------------- -----------------------
  **Outcome**             **What Happens**        **Next Step**

  **Called Away**         Stock rose above strike Sell shares at profit,
                                                  restart Phase 1

  Expires Worthless       Stock stayed below      Keep premium, sell new
                          strike                  call
  ----------------------- ----------------------- -----------------------

TERM STRUCTURE CHECK (TIMING SIGNAL)

OTS4 research shows term structure predicts volatility direction. If
back-month IV is higher than front-month IV (contango), IV tends to drop
over time - favorable for selling premium. This is a timing enhancement,
not a hard filter.

  ----------------------- ----------------------- -----------------------
  **Term Structure**      **Condition**           **Action**

  **Contango**            Back-month IV \>        **Proceed with trade
                          Front-month IV          (favorable)**

  **Backwardation**       Back-month IV \<        **Wait or reduce
                          Front-month IV          position size**

  Neutral                 Difference \< 2%        Use other factors to
                                                  decide
  ----------------------- ----------------------- -----------------------

*How to check: Compare ATM put IV for your target expiration (\~30-45
DTE) versus the next month out (\~60-75 DTE). If the further-dated IV is
higher, you have contango.*
